## Color Psychology

| Color | Feelings |
|-------|----------|
| Orange | Energy, enthusiasm, creativity |
| Blue | Trust, professionalism, calm |
| Green | Growth, health, freshness |
| Purple | Luxury, creativity, mystery |
| Pink | Playfulness, romance, softness |
| Yellow | Happiness, optimism, attention |
