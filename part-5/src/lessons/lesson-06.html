How It Works

Types of Social Proof

  • Logo Strip - "Trusted by X" shows recognized names
  • Metrics - Numbers prove scale and success
  • Testimonials - Customer quotes build trust
  • Case Studies - Detailed success stories
  • Reviews - Third-party validation

Testimonial Best Practices

  • Include a real photo or avatar
  • Add name, title, and company
  • Focus on specific, quantifiable results
  • Keep quotes authentic and relatable

Metrics That Convert

  • Customer count (10K+, 1M+)
  • Satisfaction rate (99%, 4.9/5)
  • Time saved (10x faster)
  • Revenue impact (2x conversions)
