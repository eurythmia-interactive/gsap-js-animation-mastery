Lesson 09 Part 5: Landing Page Design

Bold/Statement Design

Bold landing pages use oversized typography, high contrast, and dramatic visuals to make an immediate impact. This approach works for brands that want to stand out and make a memorable impression.

How It Works

Bold Design Characteristics

  • Oversized typography - 8xl, 9xl, or custom clamp() sizes
  • High contrast - Black background, white text
  • Single word/phrase - One powerful statement
  • Dramatic visuals - Large, impactful imagery
  • Minimal content - Restraint creates impact

Typography for Impact

  • text-[10rem] - Massive statement text
  • tracking-tighter - Tighten large type
  • leading-none - No extra line height
  • font-black - Heaviest weight available

When to Use Bold Design

  • Creative agencies and portfolios
  • Bold, youthful brands
  • Product launches
  • Event promotions
  • When you want to be remembered
