How It Works

Hero Section Anatomy

  • Navigation - Logo, key links, CTA button
  • Badge/Tag - Optional attention grabber
  • Headline - Value proposition with accent color
  • Subheadline - Elaborates on the headline
  • Primary CTA - Most important action
  • Secondary CTA - Alternative action
  • Social proof - Trust indicator (optional)
  • Visual - Product screenshot or illustration

Attention Grabbers

  • Use a badge/tag above the headline
  • Highlight key words with accent color
  • Include numbers or specific claims
  • Add urgency with time-sensitive offers

CTA Placement

  • Primary CTA should be above the fold
  • Use high contrast with surrounding elements
  • Provide secondary option for hesitant users
  • Include trust signals near CTAs
