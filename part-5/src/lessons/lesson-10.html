Product-Focused Layout

Product-focused landing pages lead with the product itself—screenshots, demos, or rich visuals take center stage. This approach works best for SaaS products, apps, and digital tools where seeing the product is believing.

How It Works

Product-Focused Characteristics

  • Product screenshot hero - Large, prominent product visual
  • Floating UI elements - Annotations, badges, highlights
  • Feature grid below - Quick scan of key features
  • Tech-forward aesthetic - Dark theme, clean lines
  • Subtle shadows - Depth for the product mockup

Screenshot Best Practices

  • Use realistic content, not placeholder data
  • Add subtle shadows for depth
  • Include browser/app chrome for context
  • Consider rounded corners matching your brand
  • Add floating elements to highlight features

Grid Layouts

  • grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 - 2 columns on tablet+
  • grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-3 - 3 columns on tablet+
  • gap-6 or gap-8 - Consistent gaps
  • Use items-center to align content vertically
