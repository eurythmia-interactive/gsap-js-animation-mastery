Key Takeaways

Putting It All Together

  • Consistent spacing - Use a spacing scale throughout
  • Typography hierarchy - Playfair for headlines, Inter for body
  • Clear CTAs - Primary and secondary actions
  • Social proof - Logos, metrics, testimonials
  • Mobile responsive - Stack on mobile, side-by-side on desktop

Complete Landing Page Checklist

  • □ Navigation with logo and CTA
  • □ Hero with headline, subheadline, and CTA
  • □ Trust signals (logos, metrics)
  • □ Value proposition or features
  • □ Social proof or testimonials
  • □ Final CTA section
  • □ Footer with links
