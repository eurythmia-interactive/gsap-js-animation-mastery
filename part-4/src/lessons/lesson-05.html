Common Flex Patterns

HTML 
<!-- Center everything -->
<div class="flex items-center justify-center">

<!-- Space between, vertical center -->
<div class="flex items-center justify-between">

<!-- Vertical stack -->
<div class="flex flex-col">

<!-- With gap -->
<div class="flex gap-4">

Flexbox Reference

justify-* (Main Axis)

  • justify-start - Default, items at start
  • justify-center - Items centered
  • justify-end - Items at end
  • justify-between - First/last at edges
  • justify-around - Equal space around each
  • justify-evenly - Equal space between all

items-* (Cross Axis)

  • items-start - Align at start
  • items-center - Align at center
  • items-end - Align at end
  • items-stretch - Default, stretch to fill
  • items-baseline - Align at baseline
